# ‚öôÔ∏è¬†Technical Explanations

---

At the core of GCP is its high-speed network infrastructure that connects its various services and allows for high-speed data transfer. GCP offers a variety of computing services such as App Engine for PaaS, Compute Engine for IaaS, and Kubernetes Engine for containerization. Its storage services include persistent disks, cloud storage for object storage, and a fully-managed, relational database service called Cloud SQL. GCP also offers machine learning and AI services, data analytics services, and many other specialized services for various needs.
